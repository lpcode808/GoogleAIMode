What is Gemini AI Mode?

Google describes AI Mode as a new generative AI experiment in Search. It’s designed to help people explore queries with more nuance and constraints, using deep information retrieval to point you to helpful web content.

It’s still experimental, rolling out as an opt-in experience in Labs. When confidence is low, AI Mode may fall back to standard web results instead of an AI response.

Source: Expanding AI Overviews and introducing AI Mode
